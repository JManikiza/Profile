My Inspirations

People

Working in such big tourist attracted areas brings people from all over the world, all walks of life. Meeting and helping a Shaman in Ecuador showed me things I thought impossible and treated everyone with sincere respect and joy, it really opened up my mind and my heart.

Sci-fi

All my life growing up: androids, robots, spaceships, aliens, bacterias, holograms, they all shape what I see in things for example whilst I was looking at a cloud it looked more like a gigantic mental manta ray, menacing in colour and was lit from underneath with a red sky.

Minimalism

I do rather enjoy none cluttered elements in my life be it at home or a website. Everything should be easy to find and concise.
